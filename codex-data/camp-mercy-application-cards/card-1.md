# Men's Camp Mercy Program Application

## <b>PLEASE NOTE:</b>
## <b>CAMP MERCY IS A PROGRAM FOR CLEAN AND SOBER SINGLE MEN WHO ARE 18 YEARS OLD OR OVER.</b>

<p> Camp Mercy is intended to be a safe place for people experiencing homelessness to get themselves out of homelessness and become self-sufficient. We will teach new skills and help our residents organize their lives.</p>

<p>We accept individuals that don't want to live on the streets and can follow the program guidelines without resistance with staff or the case manager.</p>

<p>As a resident, you agree to:</p>

- Maintain sobriety
- Obtain steady full-time employment
- Live a law-abiding lifestyle
- Obtain mental health support if needed
- Follow the financial stability plan

<p>If you believe you are a good candidate for Camp Mercy please go on to read the rules and regulations and continue your application that will follow.</p>
